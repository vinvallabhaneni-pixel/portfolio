How I Work

Most of my work sits at the intersection of content and measurement. Writing a LinkedIn post without knowing if it moved referral traffic, or setting up GA4 without connecting it to what the sales team actually needs, both feel like half the job done.

In healthcare specifically, the audience splits sharply. Referral partners respond to credibility and specificity. Family caregivers respond to empathy and clarity. I keep those two voices separate and adjust by platform.

Content
Story-led or observation-led. Short paragraphs. No filler phrases, no em-dashes, no corporate hedging. Platform voice is adjusted per audience, not per template.
Paid Media
Audience segmentation first. I identify who the ad needs to reach before choosing format or copy. Budget allocation follows the funnel stage, not habit.
Analytics
I set up tracking infrastructure so that decisions have a data layer behind them. GA4 custom events, UTM discipline, and reporting that a non-technical reader can act on.
SEO
Keyword research tied to intent, not just volume. Local citation consistency, crawl health, and backlink prospecting from sources that actually matter in healthcare.
